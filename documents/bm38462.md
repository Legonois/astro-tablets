BM 38462
========

Origin: Acquired by the British Museum in 1880 \[1\].

First Published: 1955 by T G Pinches as LBAT 1420 \[2\].

Also known as ADT V Lunar Text No.6.

Chronology
----------

Years 1 through 29 of Nebuchadnezzar.
